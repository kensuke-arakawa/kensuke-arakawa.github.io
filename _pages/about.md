Hi! I am Kensuke Arakawa, a (non-TT) assistant professor at [RIMS, Kyoto University](https://www.kurims.kyoto-u.ac.jp/en/index.html). People usually call me **Ken**.

I am broadly interested in the interaction of homotopy theory and category theory. I am especially interested in techniques to analyze higher structures from more accesible ones, such as *localization*, *model categories*, and *functor calculus*. I am also interested in applying these techniques to studying manifolds.
